About Me



Developer

I've always been attracted to the computer field, which offers countless possibilities and covers a vast range of activities. This early passion led me to explore and delve deeper into the world of technology.
To this end,I decided to pursue my studies in the IT field. I began by taking a BTS in IT services for organizations, option B Software solutions and business applications at ENC (École Nationale de Commerce). This diploma provided me with a solid grounding in IT, covering aspects such as general systems operation, methodology, web programming, application development, databases, as well as various programming languages and frameworks.

I then chose to pursue my studies by enrolling in a Licence professionnelle Métiers de l'Informatique : Systèmes d'information et gestion de données at the UVSQ (Université Versailles St Quentin Vélizy-Villacoublay). This license enabled me to deepen my development skills and discover new aspects of computer science. I was able to familiarize myself with areas such as networks, IT security, Linux, Big Data, artificial intelligence, decision-making systems, project management...

Finally, to further broaden my knowledge and gain a global vision of the different IT sectors, I chose to pursue my studies by obtaining a Diplôme d'ingénieur Spécialité informatique, Parcours Informatique, réseaux, systèmes et multimédia from the CNAM (Conservatoire national des arts et métiers). This engineering course enabled me to explore a wide range of fields, including algorithms, software engineering, web/Android programming, video game programming, game design, multimedia, accounting, business management...

Throughout my educational career, I have been able to deepen my knowledge and develop a solid skills base in different aspects of IT. These courses have given me an in-depth understanding of this constantly evolving field.
